# 🍩 TUBE INVADERS
### A Phish Space Invaders Game — Inspired by the Sphere Residency, Las Vegas

![TUBE INVADERS](https://img.shields.io/badge/PLAY-INSERT%20A%20CHORD-ff6b00?style=for-the-badge&fontFamily=monospace)

## Play It

**Just open `index.html` in any browser.** No install, no server, no dependencies. One file. Works on iPhone, Android, desktop — everything.

---

## What Is This

Phish is played another residency at the Sphere in Las Vegas that I again didnt attend. During the show, a Space Invaders-style game called **Tube Invaders** appeared on the massive Sphere LED screen — complete with pixelated aliens, a desert road horizon, and the band's faces on the game over screen. 

This is a fully playable browser adaptation of that concept, built as a love letter to 30 years of Phish.

---

## Features

- 🎮 **Full Space Invaders gameplay** — alien grid, shields, UFO, escalating difficulty
- 🌵 **Sphere-accurate background** — curved planet globe, desert road, red rock canyons, pixel cacti, scrolling perspective grid
- 🐟 **Phish Fish Boss** on Level 5 — the iconic rainbow fish logo fires bubble grenades at you
- 🍩 **Fishman's Donut UFO** — shoot it for an extra life
- ⚡ **6 weapon pickups** — Spread-3, Spread-5, Plasma Laser, Triple Cannon, CK5 Laser (explodes on impact), Life Saving Gun
- ✨ **Miracle pickup** — ultra-rare, grants CK5 + full Vibe + extra life simultaneously
- 💚 **Vibe Meter** — fills as you kill aliens, drives crowd glowstick intensity
- 🎸 **Chiptune Phish soundtrack** — 10 songs synthesized via Web Audio API:
  - Title screen: Wilson
  - Levels: Possum, Llama, Runaway Jim, YEM, Tweezer (boss), Mike's Song, Stash, Bathtub Gin, Coventry, Slave to the Traffic Light
- 📳 **Haptic feedback** — phone vibrates on every kick drum beat
- 🕹️ **Virtual joystick** + fire button for mobile
- 📱 **Tilt to steer** — uses device accelerometer (iOS asks permission)
- 🎊 **Glowstick crowd particles** — the crowd goes wild as your vibe builds
- ⚡ **White light strobe** — full screen flash on CK5 pickup and boss kills
- 🌧️ **Level 9: Coventry** — rain falls, mud slows your ship. It is sad.
- 🎭 **8-bit band member sprites** on the game over screen (Trey, Fishman, Page, Mike)
- 🎵 **Random Phish quotes** on game over: "Rhombus. Just... Rhombus."

## Controls

### Mobile
| Control | Action |
|---------|--------|
| Joystick (left) | Move ship |
| FIRE button (right) | Shoot |
| Tilt device | Alternative movement |

### Desktop
| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| Arrow keys / A, D | Move |
| Space / Z | Shoot |

**Charge shot:** Hold fire for 1 second without shooting → releases a powered 3-stream burst.

---

## Weapons

| Pickup | Name | Effect |
|--------|------|--------|
| `S3` | Spread-3 | 3-way fan shot |
| `S5` | Spread-5 | 5-way wide spread |
| `PL` | Plasma Laser | Fast piercing beam |
| `3X` | Triple Cannon | 3 parallel streams |
| `CK5` | CK5 Laser | Explodes on impact → 12 radial beams |
| `♥` | Life Saving Gun | +1 life |
| `★` | Miracle | CK5 + Life + Full Vibe |

## Technical

- **Pure HTML/CSS/JS** — single file, zero dependencies, zero build step
- **Web Audio API** — all music synthesized in-browser (square wave melody + triangle bass + kick/snare drums)
- **Canvas 2D** — all rendering, pixel art sprites, bezier path fish phish  boss
- **DeviceOrientation API** — tilt controls with iOS permission handling
- **Web Share API** — native share sheet on mobilei
- **Vibration API** — haptic pulse on kick drum beats
